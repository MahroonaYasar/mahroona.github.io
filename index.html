My Senior Project: Ticketing System

The Perot Museum of Nature and Science is a large science museum that services the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex. The museum
consists of an IT team that conducts services and maintenance to the facility. The team does repairs, creates new exhibits, software fixes,
coding fixes, hardware swaps, PC rebuilds, and PC reimages. As the team is also considered the exhibit team, some other tasks include painting,
re-gluing items, and repairing items. Our client is an individual apart of the IT team that is looking to come up with a redesigned and simpler
ticketing system that will allow users to manage tickets of varying tasks quickly and easily. Additionally, to improve the level of productivity and
efficiency, implementing a priority system with quick-search abilitie will be beneficial.
